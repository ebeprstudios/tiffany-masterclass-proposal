About

The goal is to have it all,
not to do it all.

I have tried to manage it all on my own, and I have created systems and hierarchy that supported my clients and allowed me to work on my business and not in it. I am showing others how to do the same.

Once Upon a Time

From corporate to seven figures.

I went from corporate to solopreneur, to entrepreneur, to seven-figure earner teaching CEOs how to do the same.

When my career began, I worked as a broker's assistant to one of the most successful land commercial brokers in Texas. Collin County was the fastest-growing county in the country.

During my time at the company, large developments and acreages of land were listed and sold for upwards of $200 million a year. This fast-paced client-facing role gave me the opportunity to learn firsthand about land development, planning and zoning procedures, condemnation hearings, marketing, and branding for high-end commercial and retail projects. It provided me with a deeper understanding of the legal processes of conveying real estate.

Building VBS

One staff member became thirteen. In four years.

That experience created the foundation to move into risk management for a corporate bank, and eventually to launch my first company: VBS Real Estate. The real estate virtual assistant company grew from a staff of one to thirteen in just four short years, where we assist agents in transaction coordination, listing support, and marketing.

Being a self-professed business book junkie, I now train real estate agents, assistants, and entrepreneurs on the innovative and business-minded strategies I have learned over the years.
