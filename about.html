— once upon a time

From corporate to solopreneur to seven-figure mentor.

I started my career as a broker's assistant to one of the most successful land commercial brokers in Texas. Collin County was the fastest-growing county in the country at the time. While I was there, large developments and acreages of land were listed and sold for upwards of $200 million a year.

That fast-paced, client-facing role gave me a firsthand education in land development, planning, zoning procedures, condemnation hearings, marketing, and branding for high-end commercial and retail projects. It built my deeper understanding of the legal processes of conveying real estate.

That experience became the foundation to move into risk management for a corporate bank — and eventually to start my own company: VBS Real Estate. The real estate virtual assistant business grew from a staff of one to thirteen in just four short years. We support agents in transaction coordination, listing support, and marketing.

Self-professed business book junkie. Today I train real estate agents, assistants, and entrepreneurs on the strategies that actually moved the needle for me.
