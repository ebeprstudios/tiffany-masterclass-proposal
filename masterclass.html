PLAN 04 / architect
Your Host

Tiffany Haynes

My career started inside one of the top land and commercial brokerages in Texas, where I watched eight- and nine-figure deals close every quarter. The deals were impressive. The cost of getting into them — capital, risk, time — wasn't built for the life I wanted.

So I went looking for something else. Years of research, missteps, and rebuilding later, I found a model that lets me earn passively, work from anywhere, and stay home with my family. Today I teach that model to women ready to do the same.

A different kind of math. The kind that doesn't require risking everything to build something real.
